Our Story

A Legacy of Authentic Flavors

Spice Garden was born from a simple belief — that food is the heart of every family gathering. Located in the heart of Kakinada, we have been serving authentic Andhra and South Indian cuisine made from fresh, locally sourced ingredients.

Every dish tells a story of tradition, love, and the rich culinary heritage of Andhra Pradesh.
